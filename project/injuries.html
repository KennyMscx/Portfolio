Project

This platform is designed to enable athletes to report injuries sustained during sports activities, adhering to the guidelines set forth by the Olympic Committee and ensuring compliance with GDPR regulations. The data collected will serve dual purposes: facilitating research in sports medicine and supporting sports teams in injury prevention and management. Coaches and teams also benefit significantly by being able to monitor and manage athlete health more effectively, leading to better performance and reduced injury rates.
